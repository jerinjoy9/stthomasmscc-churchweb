About Our Parish

Discover our rich heritage, mission, and the values that guide our community

Our History

A journey of faith spanning over nine decades

1935 - Foundation

St. Thomas Malankara Syrian Catholic Church was established in Nalanchira, marking the beginning of our spiritual journey in this community.

1950 - First Church Building

The first permanent church building was constructed, providing a dedicated space for worship and community gatherings.

1975 - Parish Development

Major expansion and development of parish facilities, including the establishment of Sunday School and various organizations.

2000 - Modern Era

Introduction of modern facilities and technology while preserving our traditional Malankara Syrian Catholic heritage.

2025 - 90th Anniversary

Celebrating 90 years of faith, community service, and spiritual growth in the Nalanchira community.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that shape our parish community

Our Mission

To proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ through the Malankara Syrian Catholic tradition, fostering spiritual growth, community service, and unity among all believers. We strive to be a beacon of hope, love, and faith in our community.

Our Vision

To be a vibrant, inclusive parish that nurtures faith, builds strong families, serves the community, and preserves our rich Malankara Syrian Catholic heritage for future generations.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Faith

Deep commitment to our Christian faith and the Malankara Syrian Catholic tradition, fostering spiritual growth and devotion.

Service

Dedication to serving God and our community through acts of charity, compassion, and social justice.

Community

Building strong relationships and fostering unity among parishioners, creating a welcoming and inclusive environment.

Education

Promoting spiritual education and formation for all ages, helping individuals grow in knowledge and understanding of our faith.

Tradition

Preserving and celebrating our rich Malankara Syrian Catholic heritage, customs, and liturgical traditions.

Worship

Engaging in meaningful worship through the Holy Qurbana, prayer, and spiritual practices that draw us closer to God.
