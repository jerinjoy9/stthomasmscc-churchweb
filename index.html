About Our Parish

A beacon of faith, unity, and service for over 90 years

Our Rich Heritage

St. Thomas Malankara Syrian Catholic Church, Nalanchira is a vibrant and spiritually active parish that has stood as a beacon of faith, unity, and service for over 90 years. Rooted in the Malankara Syrian Catholic tradition, our church embraces a strong sense of community through active participation in liturgy, prayer groups, organizations, and social outreach.

We welcome you to be a part of our faith journey and experience the warmth of our parish family. Our church is not just a place of worship, but a home where everyone is welcome to grow in faith and fellowship.
